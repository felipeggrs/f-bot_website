Some of F-Bot's capabilities

F-Bot's main menu is the central location for accessing and configuring the bot's various features. In the main menu, users can set the bot's waypoints, targeting preferences, backpack configuration, and much more.
F-Bot uses Tibia's waypoint system to navigate through the game world. In Tibia, waypoints are special locations that players can set on the minimap to mark important or frequently visited places. Players can then use the waypoint system to quickly and easily travel between these locations.
F-Bot has the ability to heal friends at the right time. To use this feature, players need to configure F-Bot to monitor the health of their friends and use healing spells or items when needed. They can set specific health thresholds or conditions under which the bot should initiate healing.
